Prebuilt gaming PC FAQs

Below are MSY's punchy answers to the most popular questions about prebuilt gaming PCs.

How do I build a gaming PC?

With a MSY prebuilt gaming PC, you don't have to lose hours putting your rig together. Choose your own custom PC parts and pay us to put it all together. Or buy a prebuilt MSY G. Series gaming PC to match your budget and performance needs.

What do I need to build a gaming PC?

For a custom gaming PC build, you need a CPU and cooler, motherboard, RAM, storage, graphics card, desktop case and power supply. To start gaming, just add an operating system, monitor, keyboard, mouse and headset (or speakers).

What is the best gaming PC?

The best gaming PC is the one that offers the most performance for your budget. Custom gaming PCs give you more control over parts, while prebuilt ones range from cheap to powerful.

Are prebuilt gaming PCs good?

Prebuilt gaming PCs are an affordable way to buy anything from a cheap desktop to a gaming powerhouse. Bonus: you don't have to worry about botching your build or losing time putting it all together.

Is it cheaper to buy a prebuilt gaming PC or build one?

It used to be cheaper to source individual PC parts and put everything together yourself, but that's not always the case these days. Prebuilt PCs can be cheaper than a custom PC you build yourself.

Can you upgrade a prebuilt gaming PC?

You can absolutely upgrade a prebuilt gaming PC if you're confident replacing parts. To extend the life of your prebuilt gaming PC, consider upgrading RAM, storage, graphics card, cooling or even the CPU.
