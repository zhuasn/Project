Custom gaming PCs

Pick the parts of your dream PC or let MSY determine the powerpack with a prebuilt gaming PC that's built to perform.

PC part picker

If you're a savvy PC gamer, you already have the perfect PC parts in mind. Customize all your preferred PC parts. Start with a motherboard, processor, CPU cooler and RAM. Then add a graphics card, power supply, case and a power supply. Then top MSY off with a disk or two.

Prebuilt Gaming PCs

For the gamer that wants to focus on leveling up rather than picking parts, MSY offers a range of prebuilt gaming PCs that are ready to game straight out of the box. These range from entry-level to high-end rigs that will handle anything you can throw at them.

The advantages of a gaming PC over a console

PCs are completely customizable and upgradable. If you're on a budget, you can start with a basic gaming PC and upgrade components as you go. Unlike consoles, you have the freedom to swap out parts as technology advances or your gaming needs change.

Game performance and graphics

Gaming PCs offer superior graphics capabilities compared to consoles. With high-end GPUs, you can enjoy games at higher resolutions (1440p, 4K or even 8K), faster frame rates (144Hz or higher), and with more graphical details. PC gaming also offers more customization options for graphics settings, allowing you to balance performance and visuals according to your preferences.

Versatility

A gaming PC isn't just for gaming - it's a multi-purpose machine. You can use it for work, content creation, streaming, web browsing, and more. This versatility makes a gaming PC a more practical investment for many users compared to a dedicated gaming console.

Game library and backwards compatibility

PC gaming offers access to a vast library of games spanning decades, with excellent backward compatibility. Digital distribution platforms like Steam, Epic Games Store, and GOG offer thousands of titles, frequent sales, and often better prices than console games. Additionally, many PC games support modding, extending the lifespan and replayability of your favorite titles.

Input options

While consoles are primarily designed for controller use, PC gaming gives you more input flexibility. You can choose between keyboard and mouse (which offers more precision for certain genres like FPS and strategy games), or connect various controllers including Xbox, PlayStation, and specialty gaming peripherals.
